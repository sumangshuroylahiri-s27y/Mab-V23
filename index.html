About Us

MAB Incorporation was established in the year 2016 as a sole proprietorship business organization at Jalpaiguri, West Bengal. It was formed as a result of FP7-ENV-2012, ECO-India - Energy-efficient, community-based water and wastewater-treatment systems for deployment in India, funded by Department of Science & Technology, Govt. of India and European Union.

MAB Incorporation aims to demolish water related problems so that everyone should have access to safe drinking water with minimal expenses. The firm has managed to cater safe drinking water in remote forest communities by developing solar based water treatment plants. It has marked its presence in food production industries like Rice mills, tea estates.

In urban areas, it has developed water and waste water treatment plants for individual households, society, commercial units, hospitals and institutions. It has developed water ATM systems for local government municipalities.

Our Expertise

Water Treatment

MAB Incorporation designs Water Treatment Plants according to the needs of the end user/industry. Products like Metal Removal Plants, De mineralized Plants, Reverse Osmosis Plants, Compact ROs etc.

Waste Water Treatment

Customized Effluent Treatment solutions for various types of industrial waste water to suit the wide variety of effluents and to maintain efficiency. Recycle and reuse of treated effluents is profitable.

Sewage Treatment

Sewage is a resource that can be recycled for various uses like gardening, toilet flushing, car wash. Sewage Treatment Plants are specially designed for Communities, Flats and Institutions.

Rain Water Harvesting

Rainwater harvesting (RWH) is a simple method by which rainfall is collected for future usage. MAB promotes water harvesting particularly in urban areas and industries as a technological solution.

Water Recycling

MAB Incorporation designs and implements Water Recycling. Process of converting wastewater into water that can be reused for irrigation, agricultural fields or replenishing surface water.

Civil Works

With professional engineers and qualified team, MAB Incorporation has expertise in Survey, Design, Estimate, Supervision and Turnkey Civil Projects.

Swimming Pools

MAB Incorporation designs and installs state of art Filtration Systems for Swimming Pools, Leisure Pools, Lap Pools, Diving Pools, Olympic Pools, Counter current Pools etc.

Fountains

A wide variety of Fountains like Musical Fountains, Dome Fountains, Jet Fountains, Floating Fountains, Bell Fountains, Foam Fountains, Mist Fountains, Glass Fountains etc.

Solar

MAB deals with Solar EPC for rooftop power plants, irrigation pumping, lighting and micro grid solutions. Also designs Solar Based Water Treatment Plants.

Integrated Solid Waste Management

Caters to Municipal Solid Waste Management including collection, transfer, transport and disposal. Recycling systems and composting systems.

Bio Gas Plants

Surveys, builds capacities, designs, installs and promotes usage of Biogas in a sustainable way, improving the life of countrymen by bridging the energy deficit.

Community Based Drinking Water Systems

Implementing sustainable community-based drinking water treatment schemes. Setting up self-maintained drinking water systems operated by the community.
